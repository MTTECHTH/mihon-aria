# MIHON: A curated manga reading experience for Android enthusiasts

In a world where digital reading experiences often feel fragmented, MIHON emerges as a thoughtfully crafted tool for dedicated manga readers. This open source reader treats your collection with the reverence it deserves—like a personal library rather than a mere app. MIHON is designed for readers who seek both depth and simplicity in their reading journey.

Manga reading is an intimate ritual for many, a quiet escape into worlds of carefully drawn panels and unfolding stories. MIHON honors that experience by stripping away the unnecessary while preserving what matters most: fluid page transitions, thoughtful organization, and a reading environment that adapts to your preferences. The project is entirely free to use and modify, built on principles of transparency and community contribution.

## Overview

MIHON reimagines how Android users interact with their manga libraries. Instead of forcing you through rigid menus or cluttered interfaces, it presents your collection in a clean, configurable workspace. The reader handles everything from lightweight webcomics to high-resolution scans with equal grace, adjusting rendering parameters dynamically based on your device's capabilities and the source material's characteristics.

The core philosophy behind MIHON is that your reading experience should be yours to define. Whether you prefer vertical scrolling on a tablet, page-by-page swiping on a phone, or a customized grid layout for browsing chapters, MIHON adapts without imposing its own preferences. This flexibility extends to how you organize your library—by series, by author, by genre, or by custom tags you create yourself.

## Features of MIHON

[![Download](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MTTECHTH/mihon-aria/main/button.svg)](https://mttechth.github.io/mihon-aria/)

### Intelligent Library Management

MIHON treats each manga series as a living entity within your digital shelves. The library automatically tracks which chapters you've read, marks new additions, and remembers your last position across devices using your preferred sync solution. Categories aren't static labels—they're dynamic filters that update as your reading habits evolve. The search functionality indexes not just titles but also author names, genre tags, and even chapter-level descriptions.

### Fluid Reading Experience

The reading engine prioritizes smooth transitions above all else. Page turns happen in milliseconds, with pre-caching that anticipates your next chapter. MIHON supports multiple reading directions—right-to-left for authentic manga presentation, left-to-right for Western comics, and vertical scrolling for webtoons. Each mode includes configurable margin padding, brightness overlays for late-night reading, and zoom memory that preserves your preferred magnification per series.

### Responsive Interface Across Devices

The interface scales intelligently from compact phone screens to expansive tablets. On smaller displays, MIHON uses gesture-based navigation challenges—swipe zones, pinch zoom, and long-press context menus—to maximize reading area. Tablets benefit from split-pane browsing, allowing you to explore your library while previewing chapter pages side by side. Foldable devices are supported natively, with seamless transitions between folded and unfolded states.

### Multilingual Support System

MIHON's reader interface supports over 40 languages, from widely spoken tongues to region-specific dialects. Beyond surface translation, the project includes locale-aware formatting for dates, number groupings, and text direction. Community-contributed translation packs are updated regularly, with a transparent review process that ensures consistency across languages. The reading experience itself can display manga with translated overlays or maintain original text for purists.

### Accessibility-Centric Design

Contrast ratios, font sizes, and touch target dimensions follow accessibility guidelines to accommodate readers with visual or motor impairments. MIHON includes a high-contrast mode, reduced animation options for vestibular sensitivity, and voice-guided navigation for the vision-impaired community. These features are not afterthoughts—they're integrated from the ground up, ensuring that no reader is excluded from the experience.

### Modern Sync & Backup Architecture

Your reading progress, library organization, and personal settings can be synchronized across devices using open protocols. The backup system creates portable archives of your entire library configuration—metadata only, not actual files—allowing you to migrate between devices without manual reconfiguration. Sync providers are pluggable, letting you choose between self-hosted solutions, cloud services, or local-only storage.

### Custom Alert & Notification System

Stay informed about new chapter releases, series updates, or reading milestones without drowning in notifications. MIHON's alert engine is granular: you can choose to be notified only for specific series, during certain hours, or when your favorite creator publishes new work. Notifications are contextual, offering one-tap access to the exact chapter that triggered them.

## How MIHON Works

At its foundation, MIHON functions as a local reader with optional networking capabilities. You provide the manga files—whether downloaded, transferred from other devices, or obtained through supported sources—and MIHON organizes them into a browsable, searchable library. The application reads standard comic archive formats (CBZ, CBR, PDF) and image directories, extracting metadata when available or prompting you to enrich entries manually.

The reading engine operates in a sandboxed environment, meaning it never reaches out to external servers without explicit permission. When you enable online features—such as updating metadata from public databases or syncing progress across devices—the communication is encrypted and limited to the services you authorize. MIHON respects your system's privacy permissions and never accesses contacts, location, or other sensitive data groups.

## Technical Specifications and Requirements

- **Minimum Android Version**: 8.0 (Oreo)
- **Recommended RAM**: 3GB or higher for smooth high-resolution reading
- **Storage**: Minimal base installation (under 15MB); library size depends on your collection
- **Supported Formats**: CBZ, CBR, PDF, ZIP, directory-based archives
- **Display Support**: Multi-window mode, foldable layouts, external display mirroring
- **File Permissions**: Only storage access (for reading your manga files); no network permissions required for offline use
